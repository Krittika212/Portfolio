About Me

I'm a third-year Computer Science Engineering student with a keen interest in web development. From a young age, I've been fascinated by the endless possibilities that the digital world offers, and my journey into programming began with a curiosity to understand how websites are built.

Currently, I'm diving deeper into HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, honing my skills to create visually stunning and responsive web applications. I believe that web development is not just about writing code but also about crafting seamless user experiences that leave a lasting impression.

Alongside my academic studies, I actively participate in online courses, coding challenges, and web development communities to stay updated with the latest trends and technologies.
